AIMS & OBJECTIVES

  1. To form an Alumni of all students who have passed out from and Teachers of Kendriya Vidyalaya Hebbal.

  2. To create a sense of brotherhood, co-operation, mutual harmony, love and affection amongst the members of Association.

  3. To conduct various programs for the Alumni, like get-togethers, fellowship, sports, cultural programs, with an objective to bring together all the students of Kendriya Vidyalaya Hebbal and promote fellowship amongst them and their families.

  4. To create a website of the Association so that all the information can be made available for the benefit of all the members of the Association.

  5. To undertake different activities in or outside Kendriya Vidyalaya Hebbal School premises, for the benefit of School students and members like career counseling, medical checkup camp, and other similar programs as per the decision of the executive body.

  6. To undertake different programs for needy & deserving students of the school.

  7. To conduct academic meets such as Conferences, Convocations, Workshop, Seminars for the students of Kendriya Vidyalaya, Hebbal, on various subjects.

  8. To support the Teachers/staff and alumni in the event of emergencies.

  9. To collect funds as corpus of Association for charitable purposes for the benefit of citizens of this country, like helping the government in wake of natural calamities like earthquake, flood etc. and also to establish permanent corpus fund for giving donations for the treatment of needy patients suffering from terminal diseases, including ailments of heart, kidney, etc.

  10. To institute suitable award/prizes, scholarship and stipend for the needy/deserving students, and to recognize academic, professional and other achievements of the students and the Alumni.

  11. To arrange programs with help of authorities' student development programs from International countries like student exchange program, exchange of scholarship for students from abroad, etc.

  12. To facilitate and encourage Alumni to contribute towards improvement of infrastructure like Hostel, Industry interactions and any other area that the Alumni and the Kendriya Vidyalaya feel appropriate.

  13. To render assistance to students of Kendriya Vidyalaya, Hebbal, through grants, scholarships and prizes and to provide assistance in academics, placement or in any other area that is felt as appropriate by the Association and the Kendriya Vidyalaya.

  14. To collect, publish and distribute such information as may be useful to members of the Association.

  15. To provide legal and medical aid, social economic help and assistance to the needy alumni members.
